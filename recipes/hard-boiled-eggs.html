Hard boiled Eggs

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon salt.
  • A quarter cup distilled white vinegar.
  • 6 cups water
  • 8 large eggs
Directions
  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Combine salt, vinegar, and water in a large pot, & bring to a boil over high heat.
  3. Add eggs one at a time careful not to crack them. Reduce heat to a gentle boil, and cook for 14 minutes.
  4. Once cooked remove them form the hot water, place into a container of ice water or cold, running water. Cool completely, about 15 minutes.Store in refrigirator up to 1 week.
  5. Enjoy!
